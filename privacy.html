Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2025

1. Data Controller

Sports Hub is the controller of your personal data. For questions, contact: privacy@sportshub.app

2. Data We Collect

  • Account data: name, email address, password (stored encrypted).
  • Profile data: sports preferences, skill level, availability, bio, city.
  • Location data: approximate geographic coordinates (obfuscated ~150m) to show nearby games. Precise coordinates are encrypted and never exposed publicly.
  • Usage data: games created or joined, chat messages, equipment requests.
  • Device data: device type, operating system (for app functionality only).

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and operate the Sports Hub service.
  • To match you with nearby games and players based on your preferences and location.
  • To enable communication between participants of the same game.
  • To improve the app performance and features.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

4. Legal Basis (LGPD)

We process your data based on the following legal grounds under Brazil's LGPD (Law 13.709/2018):

  • Consent: location data, optional profile data.
  • Contract execution: account data necessary to provide the service.
  • Legal obligation: data retained as required by applicable law.
  • Legitimate interest: security, fraud prevention, and service improvement.

5. Location Data

Your precise location is encrypted and stored securely using Supabase Vault. Only an obfuscated approximate position (approximately 150 meters offset) is ever displayed to other users. You can revoke location permission at any time via app settings or your device settings.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Supabase: our database and authentication provider.
  • Google / Apple: only if you choose to sign in with these providers.
  • Legal authorities: when required by applicable law.

7. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. After account deletion, personal data is removed within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

8. Your Rights (LGPD)

Under Brazilian law, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data.
  • Correct inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Export your data in a portable format.
  • Revoke consent at any time.
  • Obtain information about data sharing.

You can exercise these rights directly in the app under Settings → Privacy, or by contacting privacy@sportshub.app.

9. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted storage, Row Level Security on all database tables, and secure authentication via Supabase Auth.

10. Children's Privacy

Sports Hub is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under this age.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the app.

12. Contact

For privacy-related requests: privacy@sportshub.app
